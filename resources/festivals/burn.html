Your Recognition Powers Everything:

You have 100% recognition to distribute. You decide who contributes most to your burn experience.

Mutual recognition creates shares. If you recognize someone 30% and they recognize you 20%, your mutual recognition is 20% (the minimum).

Surplus flows to mutual contributors. Extra camping space, leftover supplies, artistic skills, and creative energy automatically flow to those in your mutual recognition network.

Effects cascade through the network. Your recognition of a DJ supports their ability to throw better parties, which enhances everyone's experience, including yours.
